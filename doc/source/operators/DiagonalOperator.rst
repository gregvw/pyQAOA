DiagonalOperator
================


Diagonal operators have the matrix representation

.. math::
    D = \begin{pmatrix} d_{11} & 0      & \cdots & 0      \\
                        0      & d_{22} & \ddots & \vdots \\
                        \vdots & \ddots & \ddots & 0 &    \\
                        0      & \cdots & 0      & d_{NN} \end{pmatrix}
